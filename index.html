Royal Challengers Bangalore

Bengaluru-based Virat Kohli-led Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a part of the Indian Premier League (IPL) from Bengaluru and plays its home match at M Chinnaswamy Stadium. But in IPL 2021, all matches will be played at neutral venues, no team will play at its home venue. All teams will play at 4 out of 6 venues during the league stage. The Royal Challengers Banglore (RCB) are the franchise cricket team based in Banglore,Karnataka,that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL).The Royal Challengers have a huge and passionate fan base All over India and especially in the city of Banglore. The slogan of RCB is EE SALA CUP NAMDE.
